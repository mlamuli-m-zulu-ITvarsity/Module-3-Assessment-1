BLOODY MARY

Prep time: 10 mins

Yield: 2

Ingredients








Steps

  1. Chill a cocktail shaker.
  2. Add vodka, tomato juice, lemon juice, Tabasco sauce, and Worcestershire sauce.
  3. Add salt, pepper, and horseradish to taste.
  4. Pour into a chilled collins glass or beer mug.
  5. Garnish with a lime slice or a celery stalk. Also preferred as garnishes: pickled green beans, okra pods, and garlic stuffed olives.
