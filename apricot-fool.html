Apricot fool

Prep time: 15 mins

Yield: 4

Ingredients






Steps

  1. To make the sauce, blend the cornflour with 2 tablespoons of the milk in a saucepan with a wooden spoon. Add the remaining milk, bring to the boil and cook for 2 minutes until thickened.
  2. Keeping half an apricot aside for decoration, purée the rest with the juice in a blender or food processor. Fold into the sauce.
  3. Spoon into individual glasses, and chill for 1 hour before serving.
