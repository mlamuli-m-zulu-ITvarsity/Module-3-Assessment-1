Cherry Mousse

Prep time: 10 mins

Yield: 8

Ingredients








Steps

  1. Combine the egg, gelatine, cornflour, cranberry juice and 1 tablespoon cold water. Stir well to blend. Add the boiling water, and mix together thoroughly.
  2. Purée the cherries in a blender or food processor, add the gelatine mixture, yogurt and sugar, and blend until smooth.
  3. Chill for 4–6 hours to set. Blend again just before serving.
